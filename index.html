Brella is 100% paperless from quote to contract to claim. Plus, our online Employer Portal and flexible enrollment options make administration a snap.

Employees can use our online shopping platform to learn about Brella and customize their benefits.

Enroll via our platform or we’ll plug into yours. We work with most major benefits administration platforms.

Forget forms and faxes. Employees can file a claim via our mobile app or online member portal.

They’ll answer a few questions and upload photos of documents they have on hand from their provider visit. That’s it!

With Brella, your people can file a claim as soon as they’re diagnosed with information they may have on hand.
